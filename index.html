The life of Tom Kilburn

Born in 1921 in Dewsbury, England.

Graduated from Cambridge with a degree in mathematics and Manchester with a PhD and a DSc in Computer Research.

Join the military during WWII, doing telecommunications research.

After the war, he worked on several computer projects of historical importance, including Manchester's "Baby", Mark 1, Mark II, Atlas, and MU5.

Helped establish the first Department of Computer Science at Manchester, serving as its department head.

Retired in 1981, continuing to server as professor emeritus.

Died Jan. 17, 2001 .
